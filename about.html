Mister Moody


I am a Freelance Extraordinaire offering specialized services to entrepreneurs, microenterprises and institutions of education. Having enjoyed a distinctive career as a TEFL Strategist working for private and public institutions of education in China, Japan, and South Korea, where I facilitated more than 6,500 hours of TEFL instruction to more than 1,000 learners, I have spent the better part of the past year learning how to build web applications using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. My end-goal in this endeavor is to advance my skill-set so that I can build and manage language learning applications that focus on improving learner communication.
